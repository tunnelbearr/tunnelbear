How to Login to TunnelBear
==========================

Logging into your TunnelBear account is straightforward. Follow these general steps:

* Open the TunnelBear application or website.
* Locate the Sign In option.
* Enter your registered email address.
* Type your account password.

* Select the Login button.
* Wait for your account dashboard to load.

Once logged in, you can connect to your preferred VPN server immediately.

How to Fix TunnelBear Login Problems
====================================

If you cannot log into your account, try these troubleshooting steps.

Verify Your Email Address
-------------------------

Make sure you are using the same email address that was used during registration.

Check Your Password
-------------------

Passwords are case-sensitive. Carefully enter uppercase and lowercase letters.

Update the Application
----------------------

Older versions may experience authentication issues. Install the latest application update.

Restart Your Device
-------------------

A simple restart often resolves temporary software problems.

Check Your Internet Connection
------------------------------

An unstable internet connection may interrupt the login process.

Clear Browser Cache
-------------------

If using the website, clear cookies and cached files before trying again.

Disable Conflicting VPN Applications
------------------------------------

Running multiple VPN applications simultaneously may create connection problems.

Reset Your Password
-------------------

If you continue receiving login errors, resetting your password is often the fastest solution.
